## ✨ About

**Dropplings** is a Minecraft Java Edition mod that introduces a new family of adorable gelatinous creatures.

These curious little beings wander the world collecting dropped items, storing them inside their transparent bodies, and interacting with their surroundings in unique ways.

From peaceful companions to rare elemental variants and powerful legendary creatures, every Droppling brings its own personality, habitat, and surprises to discover.


---

# 🟢 Features

## 🫧 Droppling Creatures

Dropplings are peaceful gelatinous creatures that:

- 🌎 Explore the world.
- 🧺 Collect nearby dropped items.
- 🫧 Store collected items inside their bodies.
- 👀 Display stored items visually through their transparent texture.
- 🦘 Move around by jumping.
- ⚔️ Defend themselves when attacked.

---

## 🎒 Item Collection System

Every Droppling has an internal inventory.

Current design:

- 📦 5 storage slots.
- 👁️ Items are visible inside the creature.
- 💎 Different Dropplings may spawn carrying different items.
- ✨ Stored items can affect their appearance.

# 🔥 Special Behaviors

### 🔥 Fire Droppling

- Appears in underground areas.
- Can naturally spawn carrying coal.
- Emits light to help players explore caves.

### ❄️ Ice Droppling

- Appears in snowy environments.

### 😈 Evil Droppling

- Appears in the Nether.
- A more aggressive variant of the Droppling species.
